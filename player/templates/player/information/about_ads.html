At ML Music, we use advertisements to support our platform and provide you with free access to millions of songs. We strive to ensure that ads are relevant, non-intrusive, and respectful of your experience. This page explains how ads work on ML Music and how you can manage your ad preferences.

1. Why Do We Show Ads?

ML Music offers a free tier that allows users to enjoy music without a paid subscription. To keep this service available, we display advertisements that help fund our operations and support artists, creators, and our platform.

2. How Are Ads Personalized?

We aim to make ads as relevant to you as possible. To do this, we may use:

  • Your music preferences and listening habits.
  • General location (e.g., country or region).
  • Device and browser information.
  • Interaction with ads (e.g., clicks, skips).

We do not share personally identifiable information with advertisers.

3. Third-Party Advertising Partners

We collaborate with trusted third-party ad networks to deliver ads. These partners may use cookies or similar technologies to measure ad performance and improve relevance. For more details, see our Cookies Policy.

4. How to Manage Your Ad Preferences

  • Upgrade to Premium: Subscribers of ML Music Premium enjoy an ad-free experience.
  • Adjust Your Preferences: Modify your ad settings through your account preferences.
  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: Some ads may be personalized based on your data. You can manage your ad choices via browser or device settings.

5. Reporting Ad Issues

If you encounter inappropriate or misleading ads, please report them to ads@mlmusic.com. Your feedback helps us maintain a high-quality advertising experience.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our advertising practices evolve. Please check this page regularly for updates.

For further questions, contact us at support@mlmusic.com.
