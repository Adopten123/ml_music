At ML Music, we believe that music should be accessible to everyone, regardless of ability. Our goal is to create an inclusive platform where all users can enjoy their favorite music seamlessly. This page outlines our commitment to accessibility and the steps we are taking to improve the user experience for individuals with disabilities.

1. Our Commitment to Accessibility

  • Providing an easy-to-navigate interface for all users.
  • Supporting assistive technologies like screen readers and keyboard navigation.
  • Ensuring high contrast modes and customizable visual settings.
  • Offering captioning and lyrics for a more inclusive music experience.

2. Features for Accessibility

  • Keyboard Navigation: Navigate ML Music using tab and arrow keys.
  • Screen Reader Compatibility: ML Music supports popular screen readers.
  • High Contrast & Color Customization: Adjust contrast settings.
  • Subtitles & Lyrics: Enjoy synchronized lyrics and captions where available.
  • Accessible Audio Controls: Easily adjust volume, playback speed, and equalizer settings.

3. Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we are actively working to:

  • Expand AI-generated audio descriptions for better usability.
  • Improve voice command support for hands-free control.
  • Enhance mobile accessibility across all devices.
  • Continuously gather user feedback to improve ML Music for everyone.
