ML Music Safety and Privacy

At ML Music, we prioritize the safety and privacy of our users. We are committed to ensuring a secure and trustworthy experience while protecting your personal information.

1. Data Protection and Privacy

  • Personal Information: We collect and store only the necessary information required to provide our services. Your data is never sold to third parties.
  • Encryption & Security: We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your data from unauthorized access.
  • User Control: You have full control over your personal data, including the ability to update or delete your account information at any time.

2. Safe User Experience

  • Content Moderation: We actively monitor and remove any content that violates our policies, including offensive or illegal material.
  • Reporting and Blocking: Users can report inappropriate behavior or block other users to ensure a safe listening experience.
  • Parental Controls: For younger users, we offer parental control settings to restrict explicit content.

3. Third-Party Services

ML Music may integrate with third-party services for enhanced functionality. These services adhere to strict privacy policies, ensuring that your data remains protected.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party services they choose to interact with.

4. Compliance and Legal Standards

We comply with global data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA, ensuring transparency and legal compliance.

If you have any privacy concerns, you can reach out to our Data Protection Officer at privacy@mlmusic.com.

5. Updates and Policy Changes

We continuously improve our safety and privacy measures. Any changes to our policies will be communicated through updates on this page. Please review this section regularly to stay informed.

For any questions regarding safety and privacy, please contact us at support@mlmusic.com.
